Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is Battle Boards - Ludo?
Battle Boards is an exciting mobile version of the classic Ludo board game, featuring unique gameplay modes, rewards.
Q2: Where can I download the game?
You can download Battle Boards - Ludo from the Google Play Store.
Q3: How do I play Battle Boards - Ludo?
The game follows the traditional Ludo rules: roll the dice, move your pieces, and race to get all your tokens to the finish line before your opponents.
Q4: How to buy BB coins?
Users can buy BB coins through QR code or UPI id provided in deposit within the wallet. Payment gateways will be added over the due course of time.
Q5: What is the price of BB coins?
100 BB coins are equivalent to 1 INR.
Q6: What are the additional charges during deposit and withdrawal from wallet?
GST is applicable during coin purchase and TDS is deducted as per applicable by Government of India. As per government notification w.e.f 01.10.2023, 28% GST is applicable on deposit and 30% TDS is applicable on net winnings. The threshold of Rs.10,000 will not apply in this case.
Q7: How to get invoices for deposit, withdrawal and TDS certificates?
The above will be available to users in Transaction History within wallet in Battle-Boards mobile application.
Q8: How to participate in matches and tournaments?
Matches can be created by any user by selecting the game from Home Page in the app. While Tournaments can be created by promoters only using their BB pin sent to their email id at the time of upgrade from user to promoter. Any User can join any match or tournament from Matches and Tournaments tab on Home Page. Once a user joins match or tournament, the game amount gets deducted from user’s wallet. Game amount is non-refundable if user fails to participate in the match/tournament after joining.
Q9: How to withdraw BB coins from wallet to bank account?
BB coins from wallet cab be withdrawn to bank account by sending withdrawal request from ‘Withdraw’ tab in Wallet using BB pin sent to email during sign-up. Coins from ‘Earn’ and ‘Deposit’ sections in wallet can be withdrawn by users whereas coins in ‘Bonus’ can be used to participate in games and ‘Earn’ BB coins which can then be withdrawn to respective bank account.
